Determined Executive

For 26 years, Bill worked at National Grid, Inc. from which he retired in 2010. Rising to the rank of Senior Vice President of Human Resources, he conducted a number of very successful labor negotiations, served on the international Human Resources Leadership Team for National Grid, and played a major role in the merger of New England Electric Services and National Grid.

Dedicated Family Man

In his 26 years as a father of four, Bill has always made a concerted effort to support his family and develop his children with the morals and values necessary to succeed independently in the 21st-century. Among his greatest accomplishments as a father is having captured almost every birthday, holiday, and special occasion on video. Now Bill spends his days converting 26 years of family videos to a more accessible and digital format in order to ensure he is able to embarrass his children with the incredible arsenal he has amassed.
